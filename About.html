About Our Unique Moving Services

Here at Orbital Moving Services, we believe that great moving services should be capable of safely transporting objects on a worldwide scale, . So, we took that thought to a whole new level.
After the 2nd Allied Victory against the Soviets, the entire network of Soviet magnetic satellites was decommissioned and sold at an auction. Since we won that auction, we decided to open a Moving Services Company in Chile (where the satellite command center is located) and utilize it to provide fast and secure!

How it works: You fill the form (including always in the description the coordinates of your location and the coordinates of the location you want your stuff to go) at our Tasks Management page. Then we come to pick up your stuff (using one of the containers we send as a personnel transport) and place them inside specialized magnetic containers (capable of withstanding space conditions and orbital re-entry). After that, we use one of our satellites to lift the containers into space, set the coordinates to the target location you provided, and the satellite sends the containers there, by using gravity. Those specialized containers use two GPS-handled parachutes that open after reaching a certain altitude, ensuring your possessions arrive at the appointed location unscathed. Once the container has reached the target location, we, who have travelled to the same location using our personnel transportation container, unload your possessions to that location.
After the task has been completed (the object transporting containers have been emptied out of their contents), we retrieve our containers, so that they are reused again in the future.
