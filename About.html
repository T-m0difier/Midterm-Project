About Our Unique Burger Joint

Here at Orbital Burgers, we believe that great fast-food services should also include a great food delivery system, capable of delivering orders worldwide. So, we took that thought to a whole new level.
After the 2nd Allied Victory against the Soviets, the entire network of Soviet magnetic satellites was decommissioned and sold at an auction. Since we won that auction, we decided to open a burger joint in Chile (where the satellite command center is located) and utilize it for food delivery purposes!

How it works: You make an order, we produce that order, and place it inside a specialized magnetic food container (capable of withstanding space conditions and orbital re-entry). Then, we use one of our satellites to lift the order into space, set the coordinates you provided, and the satellite sends the container to your location, by using gravity. That specialized container uses two GPS-handled parachutes that open after reaching a certain altitude, ensuring your order arrives at your location unscathed.
After each order, the containers are returned back to us the same way they were brought to you in order to be reused for future orders.

Purpose: Our goal is to provide and deliver great quality fast-food in a worldwide scale, while also reducing the time a hyper-atlandic (or pacific) order would take in order to reach it's destination down to a maximum of 30 minutes! (45 during holidays)

Features:

  • Innovative Global Delivery Method: We use magnetic satellites to deliver orders quickly, efficiently and securely worldwide.
  • Precision According to Input: Our satellites utilize coordinates (provided by you), as the delivery drop-off point.
  • Quality Fast-Food: We make our food with top quality ingredients and we focus on bold flavors, so that we make your meal unforgettable.
  • Goodies Gallore!: From signature burgers, to a variety of sides, drinks and desserts, we have something for everyone. For more information, check our dedicated Menu.
